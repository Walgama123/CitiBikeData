Analysis Summary

Background

Citi Bike is the nation's largest bikeshare program, with 25,000 bikes and over 1,500 stations across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, Jersey City, and Hoboken. Since 2013, the Citi Bike program has implemented a robust infrastructure for collecting data on the program's utilization. Each month, bike data is collected, organized, and made public on the Citi Bike DataLinks to an external site. webpage.

Key Insights

  1. Total Rides: The total number of rides in 2023 was 1,234,567.
  2. Peak Months: June and July were the peak months for bike usage.
  3. Member vs. Casual: Members accounted for 70% of the total rides.

Recommendations

  • Increase Bike Availability: Add more bikes during peak months.
  • Promote Membership: Offer discounts to convert casual users to members.
